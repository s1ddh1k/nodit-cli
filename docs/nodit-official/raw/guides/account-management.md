## 1. Changing Account Information

1. Navigate to the [Profile] tab in Lambda256 ID. You can check your registered account information.

   
2. Click the [pencil] emoji to change Profile Name and Company Name.
3. Enter the information to change in the popup window and click the [Change] button.
   * Profile Name can be entered with 2-20 characters excluding special characters.
   * Company Name can be entered with 1-50 characters including special characters.



:::info 👍 Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I change my Email?**

A: No, you cannot change the email you signed up with.

**Q: What information can I receive via Email?**

A: Notice, Invoice, Receipt, Newsletter, etc. are sent to the registered email.
:::

## 2. Deleting Account

:::warning 🚧 Once account deletion is completed, it cannot be reversed. Please proceed with account deletion carefully.

When you delete your account, the following restrictions occur:

1. You cannot log in to your account.
2. All personal information and assets contained in the account cannot be recovered.
3. You cannot sign up with the same account for 30 days from the date of deletion completion.
4. The Crypto Balance remaining in the account will automatically expire upon deletion and cannot be recovered. Please make sure to complete Balance withdrawal before deleting your account.
:::

1. Navigate to the [Profile] tab in Lambda256 ID. You can check your registered account information.



2. Click the [Delete Account] button.
3. In the [Delete Account] popup window, check the notice about data being unrecoverable upon deletion, then enter your password. Clicking the [Delete] button will immediately complete the deletion.

   

:::info 👍 Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I withdraw after requesting account deletion?**

A: When a customer requests deletion, deletion occurs immediately, and withdrawal from deletion is not possible.

**Q: I can't delete my account?**

A: To delete your account, you must have no active paid plan subscriptions and no outstanding charges at the time of deletion request.

* [Resolving Outstanding Charges](/guides/outstanding-charges)
* [Canceling Subscription](/guides/subscription)
:::
