## What is Credit?

Credit is a type of point that can be used for all Nodit charges, automatically paid to users through specific events. Credit can be applied to all charges incurred while using Nodit services such as plan purchases and Auto-scaling, and 1 Credit has a value of $0.01 (₩15). Credit can be used in units of 1 Credit, and is applied up to the maximum limit of the payment amount within the Credit limit held at the time of payment.

:::info 📘 Credit Application Information
* Credit can only be used within Nodit services, and once applied, Credit cannot be canceled or refunded.
* If users use coupons and Credit simultaneously, payment is deducted in the order of **Coupon → Credit → Payment method registered in Payment Method**.
:::

## How can I use Credit?

Users who hold Credit automatically use Credit when making payments. Users can check the amount of Credit currently held and the history of Credit used through the Nodit console. The detailed method is as follows.

1. Click the profile area at the top right of the console to click the [Credits] menu, or click the [Credits] menu at the bottom of the left menu to navigate to the Credit management screen.

   

<br />

2. You can check your held Credit and usage history on the Credit page.
