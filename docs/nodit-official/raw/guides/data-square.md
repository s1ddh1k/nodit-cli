## Nodit's Blockchain Data Pipeline, Datasquare
Datasquare is a data service that makes the blockchain datasets indexed by Nodit over years of expertise available to any Web3 contributor. The Datasquare pipeline aims to normalize, structure, and index the massive amounts of data generated across various public blockchain networks, making it easily accessible and usable by anyone. The processed data is delivered in various forms—dashboards, query tools, data snapshots, and more—wherever it is needed. Whether you're a developer, analyst, or enterprise looking to leverage blockchain data, visit the [Datasquare](https://datasquare.nodit.io/) homepage now.

### Key Features of Datasquare

* **Normalized Data**\
  Datasquare provides blockchain data in a standardized format, making complex blockchain data easy to understand and use. Anyone can effectively access the data structure by referencing the defined Data Schema.
* **Multichain Support**\
  Datasquare collects data from a variety of public blockchain networks, including Ethereum, Polygon, Arbitrum, and Aptos. This allows you to integrate and analyze data collected from multiple networks that share a similar Data Schema.
* **Powerful Indexing**\
  Through Nodit's proprietary indexing technology, Datasquare provides the ability to rapidly search and query vast amounts of blockchain data.
* **Real-time Data Access**\
  Datasquare delivers blockchain data in real time, enabling users to perform analysis and make decisions based on the most up-to-date information.
* **Customizable Dashboards**\
  Datasquare allows users to build customized dashboards based on the data they need. This lets users derive their own business insights and monitor data in real time.

### Using Datasquare

* **For Developers – DApp Development and Management**\
  Through Datasquare, DApp developers can easily collect data from various blockchain networks and optimize their applications accordingly. Real-time data access and powerful indexing capabilities help maximize DApp performance.
* **For Analysts and Investors – Market Analysis and Investment Strategy**\
  Analysts and investors can use Datasquare to analyze market trends and monitor key metrics in real time through customized dashboards. This enables more accurate and timely investment strategy development.
* **For Enterprise Customers – Blockchain Data Management**\
  Datasquare helps enterprises efficiently manage and analyze blockchain data without building a separate data processing system. By integrating and analyzing data collected from multiple networks, businesses can derive actionable insights and apply them to their decision-making processes.
