## What is the Referral Program?

Nodit's Referral Program is a **friend referral program** where both new subscribers who sign up through a Nodit subscriber's recommendation and existing Nodit subscribers can receive Credit benefits. Don't miss the opportunity to try Nodit's paid plans for free!


## Referral Program Participation Conditions and Reward Information

| Participation Condition | Reward Account | Reward |
| :-------------- | :---------------- | :------------- |
| Friend Invitation | Referrer (Referral Code Issuer Account) | **$10 Credit** |
| New Sign-up | Referee (New Subscriber) | **$25 Credit** |
| New Subscriber Paid Plan Subscription | Referrer (Referral Code Issuer Account) | **$50 Credit** |

### Friend Invitation 👫

Share your referral link and when a friend completes new sign-up through the link, friend invitation is successful! As soon as your friend completes new sign-up, $10 Credit is paid to your account.

### New Sign-up 🎉

When you sign up for Nodit through a referrer's referral link, $25 Credit is paid to the newly signed-up account immediately upon sign-up.

### New Subscriber Paid Plan Subscription 💳

When a friend who newly signed up through the referrer's referral link subscribes to Nodit's paid plan (Developer Plan or higher) and maintains it for 60 days or more, an additional $50 Credit is paid to the referrer after 60 days. Applied only once per new subscriber.


## How to Participate in Referral Program

### Referrer - Sharing Referral Link

1️⃣ **Navigate to My Profile:** Check the profile area at the top right of the console and click the [Profile] menu, or click the [Profile] menu at the bottom left to navigate to your profile screen.

2️⃣ **Check and Share Referral Link:** You can check your referral link in the My Referral Link area. You can click the copy icon to copy the link and share it with friends. As soon as a friend who received the link completes new member sign-up through that link, you can receive Referral Program participation benefits.


### Referee - Sign up by Clicking Referral Link

1️⃣ **Access via Referral Link:** **Click the referral link shared by a Nodit member** to navigate to the Nodit sign-up screen.

2️⃣ **Sign up:** On the sign-up screen as shown below, you can sign up with an email account by directly entering your name, email address, and password, or sign up with a Google account by clicking the [Continue with Google] button at the bottom. As soon as you complete sign-up by entering a valid email Verification Code, you can receive Referral Program participation benefits.




## FAQ

### Q. What is the concept of Credit paid as a referral reward?

Credit is a type of point that can be used for all Nodit charges and is automatically paid to users through events. Detailed information about Credit can be found in [Credit](/guides/credit).

### Q. Where can I check the Credit I own?

Credit is automatically paid to the customer account when payment conditions are met. You can navigate to the Credit management screen by selecting the [Credit] menu from the profile My Menu at the top right of the console to check.

### Q. Can the referral event be changed?

The Company may change or terminate the referral program without prior notice. However, Credits already issued are valid according to the issued terms and conditions.
